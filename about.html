My Adventure

I am a web developer because I enjoy learning and wanted to combine it with my love of computers. I am a graduate of CareerFoundry's intensive career-changers UI Program with a specialization in animation, as well as multiple courses for HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more. See my LinkedIn Profile for specific classes, certifications, and skills.

In the past, I have been a Subject Matter Expert in Comcast's Technician Center of Excellence. While assisting technicians, I routinely located pain points and created or provided ideas for processes to improve the customer and employee experience. My team and I was awarded Comcast's ACE Award for upgrading our department's knowledgebase to the newest Microsoft Sharepoint version. This change increased agent usability & integrity with better design and complete, accurate information, while paving the way for additional features to be introduced in the future.
In an effort to ensure the knowledgebase stayed true to these efforts, a style sheet and guide were created to ensure future editors adhered to the standards that were created.
In addition, I increased the customer experience by self-teaching new programs to create a ticketing tracker to improve how we handle escalated service issues, and iterated upon it to improve the agent experience further (reducing call handle time by 10 minutes).

Peers love to work with me because I bring solutions and ideas to the table, act with integrity, and get the job done. I listen to others and consider all points and sides of the story. I can also bring a sense of humor and playfulness, because all work and no play leads to employee turnover. I am open to work in remote roles. I continue to refine my technique and increase my skillset every day, and am taking additional courses for design and development on multiple platforms. Any recommendation in courses, YouTube channels, and reading is welcome!

Outside of pursuing a career-change, I love to read books and explore the wilderness. I am a mother of two children, whom I take camping every year and search for new adventures with. If there's anything else you'd like to know or would like to connect, send me a message! I am an open book and I love to get to know people.
